DIAMOND LIST

Helping sustainability focused investors discover overlooked climate companies

Not all climate companies are ready for VC funding off the bat. Some of the most innovative and essential climate solutions require a large amount of upfront research & development and/or take time to grow their markets, necessitating a long-term investment horizon. Yet these long-game solutions may have the most potential for impact. Funding them is a critical part of combating climate change.

We are four climate activists who have spent a lot of time talking to climate investors and hearing how regularly they turn away strong teams with high potential because the opportunity is just a little too early or too risky at that time. We decided to do something about this.

Diamond List is an attempt to surface the untapped gems in climate—early-stage companies that deserve investment, but have so far been overlooked. We modeled Diamond List after Hollywood’s Black List for under-the-radar screenplays. Since 2005, the Black List has uncovered hidden gems such as ‘Argo’, ‘Juno’, ‘Slumdog Millionaire’, and ‘The King’s Speech.’ Black List films have grossed over $25 billion and have been nominated for over 240 Academy Awards, winning more than 45.

HOW THIS WORKS

You—professional investors, analysts, and angels—nominate early-stage climate companies you believe in. We share the resulting list of companies back with you; perhaps others investors have spotted a diamond for your fund! Philanthropists, non profits and early stage angels focused on climate are already lined up to start working with these startups. They’re uniquely risk tolerant and eager to take notes from you.

DIAMOND LIST CRITERIA

  1. Investment Worthy. You believe in this idea, the team, and the potential for impact.
  2. Climate Focused. Addressing climate change in some way, shape, or form is core to the business.
  3. Early Stage. The earlier, the better. We are principally seeking to catalyze investment in companies where <$2M and as little as $100K would make a meaningful difference.

We accept nominations with or without revenue, anywhere in the world.

So tell us—who are the diamonds in the rough?
